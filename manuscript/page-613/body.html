GREGORYS MINSTER, KIRKDALE, BY REV. F. W. POWELL, VICAR. THE TRANSLATION IS AS FOLLOWS:

ORM GAMALS SON BOUGHT S. GREGORYS MINSTER WHEN IT WAS ALL BROKEN DOWN AND FALLEN AND HE LET IT BE MADE ANEW FROM THE GROUND TO CHRIST AND S. GREGORY, IN EDWARDS DAYS, THE KING, AND IN TOSTIS DAYS, THE EARL.

THE CENTRAL PANEL HAS ON THE DIAL

THIS IS DAYS SUN MARKER

AT EVERY TIME.
